# Dobble Card Generator Upload your own images and generate printable Dobble / Spot It cards as a PDF. ## How to use 1. Open the site 2. Choose a game size (Small / Medium / Large) 3. Upload the required number of images (PNG or JPG) 4. Click **Generate Cards** 5. Download the PDF and print on A4 ## Game sizes | Size | Images | Cards | Per card | A4 pages | |--------|--------|-------|----------|----------| | Small | 7 | 7 | 3 | 2 | | Medium | 13 | 13 | 4 | 4 | | Large | 21 | 21 | 5 | 6 | ## Privacy Everything runs in the browser. Images never leave your device. ## Debug mode Add `?debug=true` to the URL to show the debug button. Debug mode labels every image on every card so you can verify each pair of cards shares exactly one image. ## Stack - Vanilla HTML / CSS / JS - [jsPDF](https://github.com/parallax/jsPDF) for PDF generation (loaded from CDN) - Projective plane algorithm for card generation - Hosted on Cloudflare Pages (auto-deploys from GitHub)
